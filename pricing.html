Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits?

Credits are used each time you perform a virtual try-on. One credit equals one try-on session. Credits never expire on subscription plans.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do credits roll over?

Credits on subscription plans reset each billing period. One-time pack credits never expire and can be used whenever you need them.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
