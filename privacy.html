Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 7 December 2025

Welcome to Peek The Look, operated by the development team ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile app or website (collectively, the "Services"). By using Peek The Look, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our Services, you may provide:

  • Account Information: email address, password, username, and payment details.
  • Photos & Images: model photos (which may include identifiable persons) and garment images you upload for AI try-on generation.
  • Subscription & Billing Data: purchase history, transaction IDs, plan type (handled by Apple's App Store).
  • Support Communication: messages or feedback you send us.

1.3 Generated Content & Metadata

We process your uploaded model and garment photos using our AI models to generate "try-on" results. These generated images and the underlying data are handled as follows:

  • Storage: Generated images are stored to allow you to access your try-on history.
  • Research & Improvement: We may use anonymized, aggregated data, and non-identifiable metadata to improve our AI models and algorithms.
  • Metadata Retention: We may retain certain non-image metadata (e.g., file size, upload time, usage logs) for security, fraud, and abuse prevention purposes.
  • Deletion: You can delete your generated images anytime within the app.

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the Services (e.g., generate try-on images).
  • Manage your account, billing, and subscriptions.
  • Communicate with you (service updates, technical notices, or support).
  • Improve our algorithms, performance, and user experience.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Providers

This section explains how your images are processed using external AI services and our policy regarding model training.

  • No Model Training by Us: We affirm that we do not use your uploaded photos, generated try-on images, or any associated data to train, fine-tune, or improve any of our own AI models.
  • AI Service Partner & Data Protection: We currently utilize the paid-tier Google Gemini API for the computational image processing required to generate try-on results. When you use a paid service like this API, Google's terms provide strong privacy protections:
    • Google's Policy: Google does not use the inputs (your photos/prompts) or the outputs (the generated try-on images) to train or improve its generative AI models.
    • Abuse Monitoring: However, Google, as the service provider, may temporarily log and retain inputs and outputs for a limited duration (e.g., up to 55 days) for abuse monitoring, security, and policy enforcement purposes, in accordance with their own terms of service. This data is separate from model training data.
  • Third-Party Responsibility: Our use of Gemini and any other third-party AI service is subject to their respective privacy and security policies, which we encourage you to review. We do not take responsibility for the data handling practices of third-party providers outside of the strict contractual terms and confidentiality agreements we have with them.
  • Future Changes: We may change our underlying AI service provider(s) in the future. We will ensure that any future provider maintains comparable contractual restrictions preventing the use of your images or data for training their models. We will update this Privacy Policy to reflect any material changes to our AI providers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:
