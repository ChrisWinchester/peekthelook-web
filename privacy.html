Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06 December 2025

Peek The Look ("we", "our", or "us") provides an AI-powered virtual try-on service that allows users to upload photos and preview clothing on themselves. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and the rights you have over your data.

By creating an account, uploading photos, or using our app or website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in using Apple, Google, or email, we collect:

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address
  • Apple Sign-In Identifier (unique user ID)
  • Profile information you optionally add

We do not receive or store your Apple password.

1.2 Photos and Images (User-Generated Content)

To create virtual try-on images, you may upload or capture photos.

We collect:

  • The photos you upload
  • The AI-generated try-on results
  • Metadata related to processing (e.g., timestamps)

Important Clarifications

  • We do not create or store biometric identifiers, facial recognition templates, or face embeddings.
  • Photos are used only for generating user-requested results.
  • We do not use your photos for advertising or tracking.
  • Photos are stored temporarily and deleted when no longer needed for the service.

1.3 Subscription & Purchase Information

When you subscribe through the Apple App Store or Stripe, we receive:

  • Transaction ID
  • Subscription status
  • Plan type
  • Renewal or cancellation events

We never see or store your full payment card number or billing address. Payments are processed securely by Apple or Stripe.

1.4 Device & Usage Information

We may collect:

  • Device model, OS, language, and region
  • App activity (buttons tapped, screens viewed)
  • Crash data and diagnostic logs
  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

This data helps us improve performance and provide support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Authenticate your account
  • Process your photos to generate virtual try-on results
  • Store your try-on history
  • Show your subscriptions and credits

2.2 Service Improvement

We may use anonymized and aggregated data to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Understand general feature usage
  • Detect crashes or errors

Your identifiable photos are not used for AI model training without explicit opt-in consent.

2.3 Customer Support

To troubleshoot issues and help you with your account.

2.4 Legal & Security

To prevent fraud, secure user accounts, and comply with applicable laws.

3. AI Processing Clarification (Required for Apple Compliance)

Peek The Look uses AI technology to generate virtual try-on images.

We do NOT:

  • Use your photos for advertising
  • Use your photos for tracking across other apps
  • Extract biometric identifiers
  • Perform facial recognition or identity matching

AI Training / Model Improvement

Your photos are not used to train AI models unless you explicitly opt in through a clear consent screen.

If you decline, your photos are used only to generate your try-on results.

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • User photos: stored temporarily while generating results; deleted automatically after they are no longer needed.
  • Generated try-on results: stored in your account until you delete them.
  • Account information: kept until you delete your account.
  • Diagnostic logs: retained for short periods to improve app stability.

You may delete your account at any time to permanently erase your data.

5. How We Share Your Information

We share your data only with trusted service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Cloud storage providers (for temporary photo processing)
  • AI processing partners
  • Analytics & crash reporting providers
  • Apple / Google / Stripe (for subscriptions)

All third-party partners are bound by data-processing agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Cookies and Web Tracking

The web version of Peek The Look may use cookies for:

  • Authentication
  • Session management
  • Basic analytics

We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data or photos
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Object to certain uses
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

You can access these features directly in the app or contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Peek The Look is not intended for users under 16.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

If we discover that a child has created an account, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

We may process data on servers located in:

  • The European Union
  • The United States
  • Other regions where our service providers operate

All transfers follow GDPR-approved safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures like:

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud storage
  • Access control and authorization
  • Regular security reviews

However, no online service is 100% secure.

11. Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account at any time in the app.

This will permanently erase:

  • Your photos
  • Your generated try-ons
  • Your profile data
  • Your usage data

This action cannot be undone.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are significant, we will notify you through the app or email.
